about us

Training program Only comprehensive training gives the desired result , so at NEKTOP we teach everything that is necessary to achieve it. Ages, 11-25 years old.
A professional fashion photographer will talk about the basics of posing and show in practice which poses and angles are the most advantageous and spectacular.

Start your ascent to the glossy world with the NEKTOP School modeling school. In just 3 months of hard and interesting work on yourself under the guidance of professionals, you will become the standard of beauty and style.
